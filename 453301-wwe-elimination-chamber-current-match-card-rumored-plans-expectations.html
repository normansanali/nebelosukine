WWE Elimination Chamber: Current Match Card, Rumored Additions &amp; Expectations for Next Weeks PPV

August 2024 · 1 minute read

The following matches have been announced: 


Randy Orton (c) vs. Sheamus vs. Christian vs. John Cena vs. Cesaro vs. Daniel Bryan


Big E (c) vs. Jack Swagger

The Shield vs. The Wyatt Family

Batista vs. Alberto del Rio

At least one more match for the main card, if not more, is expected to be announced for the Elimination Chamber pay-per-view. A pre-show match has yet to be announced, but WWE hasn't been without one since they began airing.

, it is likely the New Age Outlaws will be made to defend the tag team championships against the Uso brothers at the PPV. This could round out the main card – there was talk of having AJ Lee defend the Divas championship against Naomi, however after the Funkadactyl was injured by Aksana it looks like plans for that match have been scrapped, or at the very least pushed back. 

Although nothing has been confirmed, the expectations are that Randy Orton will retain the championship heading into WrestleMania, going ahead with his match against Batista as planned. Creative plans for April's big event are constantly changing, according to reports, so anything is possible at this point.
