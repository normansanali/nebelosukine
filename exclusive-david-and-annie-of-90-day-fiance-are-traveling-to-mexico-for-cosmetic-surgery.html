Exclusive: David And Annie Of '90 Day Fiance' Are Traveling To Mexico For Cosmetic Surgery

September 2024 · 2 minute read

Annie Suwan and David Toborowsky from 90 Day Fiancé are taking a trip to undergo surgery. The procedure will be administrated by Dr. Jesús Flores of Chihuahua, Mexico.

An exclusive source told TheThings about what fans can expect from the couple after the makeover.

Neck Contouring

TheThings spoke with Annie and David's manager Dominique Enchinton on the matter.

The talent agency Dominton Talent House owner explained, "They (the doctors) went viral because this doctor has developed a special technique that no other surgeon has. Contouring the neck area."

"It's under the neck and chin. David's going to have a complete transformation...They're having doctors from Dubai and all over the world wanting Dr. Flores to teach them the technique."

The Cardé Clinic just received a new machine for the surgery, with which David will reportedly be one of the first patients to experience. We're not certain if this process will be filmed and made accessible to viewers.

The device is called J-Plasma and according to online sources it, "is used to turn helium gas into plasma that is then used to resurface the skin. It is used to lift, tighten and rejuvenate the skin. It can be used on any part of the body."

The Future For David And Annie

David and Annie have won over the hearts of 90 Day Fiance viewers with their genuine connection and appreciation for each other's cultures. Although they received criticism over financial hurdles in the past, the two are doing better than ever.

Dr. Flores' clinic has about 30,000 followers on Instagram and while his earlier posts received a low amount of traction, his neck procedure is attracting a brand new audience.

His comment sections are now filled with women and men from America hoping to book their own appointments. Enchinton insisted that the neck transformation will be "life-changing for David."

Until we hear back about their results, fans can stay tuned in to the couple's kitchen adventures. In addition to their cooking videos, the two are also planning the release of an exciting cookbook.
