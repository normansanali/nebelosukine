WWE Elimination Chamber: Brock Lesnar vs. Bobby Lashley Result

September 2024 · 1 minute read

The battle between and Bobby Lashley ended in chaos, as Lesnar was tossed due to DQ, causing him to launch into a huge attack in the ring.

In what was one of the more highly anticipated matches of the night, Lesnar and Lashley began fighting the moment the bell rang. After hitting some belly-to-belly suplexes on Lashley, Lesnar was then hit by a big Spear by Lashley. Later in the match, Lashley would hit another Spear after getting out of an F5 attempt, and quickly locked in the Hurt Lock on Lesnar. With nothing left to do, Lesnar hit Lashley with a low-blow, prompting the referee to immediately call the match.

After the bell rang and the match was over, an irate Lesnar then grabbed the referee. After hitting the official with an F5, Lesnar then dragged Lashley out of the ring and put him through the announce table with an F5. With the crowd chanting in shock, Lesnar then got back into the ring and delivered one final F5 to the referee before storming off and up the ramp.
