The Power Actress Anissa Matlock Wiki | Bio

Anissa Matlock (age: 32 years; born on June 9, 1990) is an actor from Augusta, Georgia, working in Hollywood since 2013. Over the years, she worked on numerous TV productions, including “Mayfair Witches,” “The Resident,” “The Walking Dead,” and “Intrepid Academy.”In 1st and 2nd seasons of Matt Nix’s show “The Gifted,” she played the role of a Porcelain Mutant.

But Anissa isn’t just limited to TV shows. She has also made a name for herself in the film industry with standout performances in movies such as “Flesh” and “Dead by Midnight.” And with dozens of short films, Anissa’s versatility as an actor is remarkable.

Most recently, Anissa’s star has been shining bright with her captivating performance in the brand new show “The Power,” which premiered on Prime Video on March 31. Audiences are in awe of her talent and are eagerly waiting for her future projects.

How Old is Anissa Matlock?

On June 9, 1990, Anissa Matlock (age 32) was born to her father, Bruce, and mother, Natalie Sivil, in Augusta, Georgia, America. She has two siblings, including a brother named Brandon and a younger sister.

Growing up as an Army Brat, Anissa lived in various states, including Hawaii, California, Texas, and Colorado. Her childhood was marked by frequent relocations, which instilled in her a love for travel and a thirst for new experiences.

Despite the challenges of constantly moving, Matlock remained focused on her education and was homeschooled from the 2nd grade until dual-enrolling in college during her senior year of high school.

Anissa’s passion for acting led her to pursue a degree in Theatre, which she successfully completed in 2012. Her dedication to her craft was evident from an early age, and she honed her skills through years of hard work and practice.

Anissa Matlock’s Acting Career Timeline

Anissa’s journey as a professional actor began in 2014 with the hit AMC show “The Walking Dead,” where she played a brief role. That year she also worked at “Intrepid Academy” and “Pepper’s Place” but as a makeup artist.

Meanwhile, she also began working on short movies; her first short film was “Welcome to the Bubs,” where she played the part of a flight assistant.

In 2017, she returned to the small screen with the TV series titled “Murder by Numbers.” It was a Geoffrey Proud-directed crime drama featuring Adele Shepherd, Michael Reagan, Brittany Goodwin, Josh Mancuso, and William Tokarsky. The same year she portrayed Porcelain Mutant in the highly appreciated “The Gifted.”

On the other hand, her filmy career also towered with her performance in projects like “Silence Her,” “The Run Saga: Breathe,” “Beast Beast,” and “Dead by Midnight.”
