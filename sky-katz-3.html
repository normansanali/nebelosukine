Career

Sky Katz made her appearance in the TV show “Home and Family” at the age of 7 only.

She got a breakthrough as a singer and rapper in 2016 with her appearance in the reality show “America’s Got Talent”, where she sang her self-composed song “Fresh and Haters”.

After this she made her live performances in numerous shows and events such as “MTV’s TRL”, “The Harry Connick Jr. Show”, “New York Knicks Halftime Show”, “New York Fashion Week’s Rookie USA Fashion Show”, “The Winter Film Awards”, “McDonald’s Houston Children’s festival and others.

In addition, she began sharing her videos and albums on YouTube. Some of her famous music works include “Haters and Fresh”, “Like This”, “Back At It”, “Long Way to Go”, “Saucy”, “Crushin” and many more. She has also sung the songs of renowned artists like Drake, Nicki Minaj, Lil Mama, and Rakim with her rap skills.

She debuted in acting in 2017 by playing the character named, “Tess” in the  Disney Channel’s hit sitcom “Raven’s Home” in which her acting was well appreciated and she started grabbing other Disney roles too.

She has made her appearance in the “Disney Channel Stars: Duck Tales theme song and “Disney Channel Stars: Legendary”. She lent her voice in the television movie “Kim Possible”.

Moreover, she will be seen as the character “Summer Torres” in the Netflix series, “Surviving Summer” in the future.
