What Was Taylor Swift And Calvin Harris' Wife Vick Hope's Relationship Like Before Hope Said She Was A Fan?

Given Harris and Swift's busy music careers and the awkward circumstances of their breakup, it seems unlikely that they would still talk.

Since Hope said that she listens to Swift when her husband isn't around, it sounds like she thinks that Harris might be upset if he knew the truth. Any Taylor Swift fan would agree that it seems impossible not to love Swift's catchy beats, vulnerable and poetic lyrics, and inspiring place in the music industry. Of course, Hope probably loves Calvin Harris' popular songs since so many of them are huge hits. But unlike Taylor Swift's most popular songs, she can listen to those out in the open.

According to People, Vick Hope hosts the BBC Radio 1 show Going Home with Vick, Katie and Jamie and admitted to loving Swift's music in an episode.

Inside Calvin Harris And Taylor Swift's Relationship

Calvin Harris and Taylor Swift dated from 2015 to 2016 for 15 months, according to InStyle, and things always seemed great between them. When they broke up in June 2016, Swift's fans were shocked since she seemed genuinely happy with him.

After Ellie Goulding, who is friends with both Swift and Harris, thought that the two should meet during the February 2015 Elle Style Awards, the two began dating sometime in winter/spring 2015. While they didn't talk much about being together, they were photographed on vacation in March 2016. They also went to the 2015 Billboard Awards.

A source told E! News that the couple broke up because "the romance just was not there anymore for them." The source said that Harris is the one who made the decision.
