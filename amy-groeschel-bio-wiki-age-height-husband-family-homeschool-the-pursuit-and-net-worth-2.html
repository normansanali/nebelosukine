Amy Groeschel Biography

Amy Groeschel is a renowned American author and public speaker who was born and brought up in Oklahoma, United States of America. She co-founded Life Church Ministry together with her spouse Pastor Craig in January 1996. Originally, the church was located in a carport in Edmond, Oklahoma.

Groeschel leads the church’s female ministry known as sisters and also organizes exhibitions for the ministry all over the nation. She also founded a women’s ministry known as Branch 15 in 2013, that features a traditional home. The system helps in enabling ladies as well as rehabilitating ladies who are displaced.

Additionally, Groeschel features on a podcast known as The Messy Table which is an affiliate of the Life Church. She has co-authored a book with her spouse Craig about marriage, From This Day Forward.
