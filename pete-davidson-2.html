Pete Davidson aka Peter Michael Davidson is an American comedian, actor, writer and producer. As of 2022, Pete Davidson’s net worth is $8 million. He is popularly known for being a cast member on the popular television sketch comedy show titled Saturday Night Live for the last 7 years.

He is also popularly known for his appearance in popular television shows like Guy Code, Failosophy, The King of Staten Island, Comedy Underground With Dave Attell, and others. He is one of the talented and promising comedians in the United States.

Pete Davidson Wiki/Biography

Born on 16 November 1993, Pete Davidson’s age is 29 Years Old as of 2022. He was born and raised in a well-settled Christian family from Staten Island, New York, United States. He holds an American by nationality and has his belief in the Christian-Catholic religion.

Pete Davidson Childhood Pic

He completed his early schooling at St. Joseph by-the-Sea High School in Staten Island, New York, Tottenville High School in New York, and Xaverian High School in Bay Ridge, Brooklyn.

After that, he enrolled himself at St. Francis College in Brooklyn Heights, New York, the United States where he completed only one semester and after that, he drops out of college to pursue his career in comedy. Since childhood, he has had an interest in comedy and has always wanted to pursue a successful career in the entertainment industry.
