Bryson Tiller 2024 Tour: Dates, Tickets &amp; More

September 2024 · 4 minute read

In a momentous announcement, Grammy-nominated artist Bryson Tiller has revealed his much-anticipated album, simply titled Bryson Tiller, alongside the rollout of his North American tour. With 30 performances scheduled for this spring and summer, fans can expect an unforgettable experience that merges Tiller's signature blend of trap and soulful R&B with fresh rap and pop elements.

Bryson Tiller's Upcoming Album & Recent Singles

Bryson Tiller’s eponymous album promises to be a deeply personal narrative that showcases the "Wild Thought" singer's evolution as an artist. It melds his captivating storytelling with innovative sounds, setting a new standard for authenticity and emotional depth. The album's lead single, "Calypso," provides a tantalizing glimpse into this sonic journey, highlighting Tiller's lyrical prowess and infectious rhythms. Accompanying the album announcement is the unveiling of its cover art, signaling the dawn of a new era for Bryson Tiller. This visual component adds another layer of intrigue to the project, inviting fans to delve deeper into Tiller's artistic world.

Bryson Tiller Tour Dates

Spanning 29 cities across North America, The Bryson Tiller Tour promises to bring Tiller's electrifying performances to fans far and wide with opening performances from Slum Tiller and DJ Nitrane. From Portland to Puyallup, Tiller will grace iconic venues with his mesmerizing stage presence. With stops in major cities like New York, Los Angeles, and Toronto, fans everywhere will have the opportunity to experience the magic of Bryson Tiller live.

The tour kicks off on May 11 at the Alaska Airlines' Theater of the Clouds in Portland, Oregon, and concludes on September 21 at the Washington State Fair in Puyallup, Washington. Along the way, Tiller will light up stages in renowned venues such as the Bill Graham Civic Auditorium in San Francisco, the Budweiser Stage in Toronto, and The Theater at Madison Square Garden in New York City.

Where To Find Tickets

BERLIN, GERMANY - APRIL 07: American singer Bryson Tiller performs live during a concert at the Astra on April 7, 2016 in Berlin, Germany. (Photo by Frank Hoensch/Redferns via Getty Images)

Tickets for The Bryson Tiller Tour will be available starting with various pre-sales on Wednesday, March 20. Additional pre-sales will run throughout the week, leading up to the general on-sale on Friday, March 22 at 10 AM local time. Fans can secure their tickets through ticketmaster.com, ensuring they don't miss out on this unforgettable musical experience. In addition to the tour, fans can also pre-order Bryson Tiller now. With its official release set for April 5, anticipation is mounting for this landmark project. As the countdown to both the album release and tour kickoff begins, fans can expect more updates and exclusive content from Bryson Tiller. Stay tuned for further details and immerse yourself in the captivating world of Bryson Tiller's music.
