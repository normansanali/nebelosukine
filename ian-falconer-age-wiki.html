Ian Falconer Death

Ian Falconer was best known as the author and illustrator of the beloved Olivia kid book series about a spry and smart young pig. He died on Tuesday 7 March 2023 in Rowayton, Norwalk, Connecticut, New York City.

The author, theatrical set, and costume designer was 63 years old at the time of his death. Falconer’s lawyer and agent, Conrad Rippy, said that his cause of death was kidney failure.

Ian Falconer Wiki

Ian Woodward Falconer was an American author and illustrator of kids’ books. He wrote and illustrated the Olivia series of kids’ books, and recorded the exploit of a young pig, a series originally conceived as a Christmas gift for his young niece named Olivia.

Ian graduated from The Cambridge School of Weston, Massachusetts, and studied art history at New York University (NYU) before transferring to the Parsons School of Design, Greenwich, New York City, and the Otis Art Institute, Los Angeles, California.
