Sukeban, an all-women's Japanese wrestling promotion, has garnered significant attention in the wrestling world with its unique approach and talented roster.

For fans keen on catching the action, here's a comprehensive guide on where to watch Sukeban wrestling events, including streaming details, event locations, and the platforms to tune in.

Sukeban Streaming Details

Sukeban has made strategic decisions to stream its events exclusively, providing fans with the opportunity to experience the excitement of its debut shows from different locations.

The promotion's choice of streaming platforms has been a key factor in making its events accessible to a global audience.

Sukeban World Premiere

The inaugural event of Sukeban was the "Sukeban World Premiere" which took place in New York City.

The event was streamed exclusively on TikTok, providing fans with a unique opportunity to witness the action live from their devices. The debut event featured a compelling lineup of matches, showcasing the talent and diversity of the Sukeban roster.

Streaming Details:

  • Date: Thursday, September 21, 2023
  • Platform: TikTok
  • Time: 8pm ET
  • Matches: The event featured five matches, including a special main event title eliminator match and other exciting matchups.

Sukeban Miami

Following the success of its debut event, Sukeban announced its Miami event, which also featured exclusive streaming details. The promotion continued its trend of partnering with popular platforms to ensure widespread accessibility for its events.

Streaming Details:

  • Date: December 6, 2023
  • Platform: CEO Gaming Twitch Channel and TikTok
  • Time: 10pm ET
  • Location: Lot 11 Skatepark in Downtown Miami
  • Matches: The event showcased an enticing lineup, including a Sukeban World Championship match and other thrilling bouts.

Ticket Information

In addition to the exclusive streaming options, Sukeban also offers limited tickets for its live events, providing fans with the opportunity to witness the electrifying action in person.

For those eager to be part of the live audience, Sukeban has facilitated the purchase of tickets through its official channels.

  • Sukeban Miami Tickets: Limited tickets for the live event in Miami were available exclusively via Dice at sukeban.com/miami. The event also included the Sukeban Street Fair, adding to the overall experience for attendees.
