Nicki Minajs Complete Dating History

September 2024 · 12 minute read

With the deck stacked against her, Nicki Minaj has made her mark as a legend in the music business by shattering stereotypes in a field that is predominately male. The rapper, who was born in Trinidad, has a long list of accomplishments to her credit, including holding the record for the longest-charting female rapper of all time (Missy Elliot previously held the distinction). Since Lil Wayne took her under his wing and helped her start her career, she has been a force to be reckoned with.

But Minaj’s fortitude goes beyond the difficulties she has faced in the rap business. The “Barbie Dreams” artist was raised in a troubled household that was filled with alcoholism and abuse, and the difficulties she had as a child motivated her to succeed. According to the woman who spoke to Rolling Stone, “When I first came to America, I would go in my room and bow down at the foot of my bed and pray that god would make me rich so that I could take care of my mother.”

Minaj has settled down with her longtime partner Kenneth Petty after realizing her ambitions. In public, they call their kid, whom they welcomed in 2020, “Papa Bear.” Many previous suitors have knocked on the doors of the “Super Bass” singer’s Barbie kingdom. We’re going into Nicki Minaj’s whole dating history (and what her ex-boyfriends have said) before her marriage to convicted sex offender Kenneth Petty.

Nicki Minaj’s tumultuous relationship with Safaree Samuels


For more than ten years, Nicki Minaj dated Safaree Samuels. Early in the 2000s, they both joined the Hood$tars rap group, but their friendship was marred by rumors of police involvement and public disputes.

In 2011, after TMZ got a police record following an incident between Minaj and a man officers allegedly identified as Samuels, rumors about difficulties in the couple’s relationship initially started to circulate. Samuels allegedly hit the rapper across the jaw and lower lip during an argument with a bag. Minaj decided against filing a complaint. Despite the fact that they were still seeing each other, their issues were far from resolved.

The couple broke up in 2014 amid rumors of jealously over Minaj’s success, which Samuels said he helped shape. The song “Bed of Lies,” which Minaj released, was widely believed to be about her unhealthy relationship with Samuels. The rapper claimed Minaj owed him money for years of assistance with her music when he filed a lawsuit against her for music credit. Samuels eventually withdrew the lawsuit, but years after their breakup, the two were still at odds in their songs and on Twitter. Rapper Meek Mill alleged that Minaj had been disloyal to him, while Samuels claimed that Minaj had been using him. He said, she said is the situation here. Samuels said in a tweet that the rapper stabbed him in 2014 while brandishing a knife. TMZ claims to have seen a video of the incident, though this has never been corroborated.
