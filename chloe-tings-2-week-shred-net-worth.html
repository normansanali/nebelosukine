Does Chloe Ting’s 2 Weeks Shred Plan Work?

In recent years, several stars have turned to Peloton for their exercise regime and others are more concerned with perfecting their workout selfie. On the other hand, any star or the average person who is looking for an intense workout that is going to make them sweat can learn from Chloe Ting.

Of course, every workout program in the world has its detractors for one reason or another. Unsurprisingly, some of the people who’ve reviewed Chloe Ting’s workout program have had some serious issues with it.

Most notably, the biggest complaint that people seem to have about Chloe Ting’s content is the title of her videos. Since Ting labels her videos things like “Get Abs in 2 Weeks”, reviewers point out that it isn’t really possible to go from average to ripped that quickly.

Putting aside the titles of Chloe Ting’s videos, another recurring complaint people have about her workouts is some people feel they are too repetitive. On top of that, some people have expressed that Ting’s workouts are too intense since they aren’t advertised as being only for already fit people.

On the bright side, virtually everyone who has reviewed Chloe Ting’s workouts agrees on one thing, if people stick to it, they will improve their level of fitness. On top of reviewers agreeing about that, Quora users have also come to the consensus that people who follow Ting’s workouts will see results.

Of course, it should go without saying that workout videos like Chloe Tings could never replace the experience of having a fitness program designed just for you. However, considering that Ting’s videos are free to watch, they certainly seem to be a great option for anyone who wants to work out on a budget.

Keep in mind, just like every other workout program, Chloe Ting’s videos won’t be effective if they aren’t paired with healthy diet choices.

How Much Money Is Chloe Ting Worth?

Born in Brunei, Chloe Ting’s family moved to Australia when she was sixteen years old. Now located in Singapore as of 2021, Ting has already experienced more of the world than a lot of people will ever see. Perhaps Ting’s globetrotting past has played a role in her international success.

No matter what the key has been to Chloe Ting’s ability to attract viewers, the fact remains that she has developed a huge fan following. For proof of that, all you have to do is look at the fact that several videos on Ting’s YouTube channel have views in the hundreds of millions.

For the top YouTubers, one of the biggest keys to their financial success is their ability to place several mid-roll ads in their videos. Unfortunately for Chloe Ting, however, fans would almost certainly be outraged if her workout videos were constantly interrupted by loads of ads.

Seemingly an extremely intelligent person, Chloe Ting has come up with a way to still profit from multiple ads. Instead of having her videos interrupted by loads of ads, Ting has made sure to keep her uploads short. As a result, Ting’s followers are expected to watch several of her videos a day and lots of ads.

On top of making money from YouTube, Chloe Ting has also managed to monetize her fan following by selling products directly to consumers. As of the time of this writing, Ting’s fans can buy her branded clothing and resistance bands on her website.

Thanks to all of her revenue streams, Chloe Ting has managed to amass an impressive fortune over the years. According to celebritynetworth.com, Ting has a $2 million net worth as of this writing.
