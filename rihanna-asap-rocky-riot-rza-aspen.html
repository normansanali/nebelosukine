Rihanna, A$AP Rocky, and Their Kids Are the Most Stylish Family on Aspen Getaway

August 2024 · 3 minute read

Rihannah and A$AP Rocky have long claimed and maintained the title of “most stylish couple”—now, they are cementing their hold on the “most fashionable family” award. For their latest outing, the pair enjoyed a getaway to Aspen with their kids, while showcasing expert cool winter layering.

The family of four was photographed shopping at luxury western-wear store Kemo Sabe on Thursday, which hopefully means we will be getting more iconic RiRi style moments in cowboy hats and boots.

BG041/Bauer-Griffin

The “Love on the Brain” singer layered a large brilliant white blazer over an oversize gray hoodie paired with light-wash baggy jeans. A maestro of high-low fashion, Rihanna elevated her edgy outfit with a touch of luxury accents, specifically in the form of massive diamond earrings and blue-tinted aviator sunglasses. As for footwear, she stepped out in options: first, tan snow boots that featured a chocolate-brown gum sole and bright blue laces; and later, stone-blue suede boots that featured a brown block heel. She styled her hair in long braids and glammed up with deep pink glossy lips, bronzy eye shadow, and black winged eyeliner.

“Praise the Lord” Rocky coordinated with his wife in the same snowshoes and gray hoodie, but gave his look a more maximalist twist, donning a long brown-and-white fur coat and metallic silver pants that shone in the light.

The rapper held their older son, 19-month-old RZA, who showcased his mom and dad’s style genes with his own suave winter ensemble: a green, yellow, and brown argyle vest over a crisp white button-down and khaki pants. He bundled up in a brown-and-tan wool coat that featured a chevron design and finished off the look with brown-and-tan Gucci socks. Four-month-old brother Riot brought a pop of color to the group with a vibrant green cardigan that featured a white design, along with dark-wash jeans, a beige stringed beanie, and matching socks.

The holiday vacation comes a few days after Rihanna’s Fenty x Puma party in Los Angeles. For the event, the fashion mogul looked incredibly cool in a shaggy lavender fur coat layered over a quilted blue button-up jacket by Chanel and a white tank top with red varsity layering. She paired the electric outerwear with faded wide-leg R13 jeans and the shoe of the night: the Fenty x Puma Creeper Phatty sneakers, which she sported in a dusty purple hue.
