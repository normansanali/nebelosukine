Kaslyn Switzer Died in a tragic car accident

September 2024 · 2 minute read

Switzer a resident of Asheboro, North Carolina was identified as the person who died in a tragic accident on Saturday, May 13, 2023.

Kaslyn succumbed to the injuries sustained from the accident. The cause of the fatal incident has not been released to the public. Friends, family, and loved ones have paid tributes to Kaslyn Switzer across social media platforms.

A Facebook user Daryn Trogdon wrote, “You never know, never. I just talked to Kaslyn yesterday. I would’ve never guessed it was the last time I would hear from her, ever. 8 years ago we become best friends, bound by laughter, good times, and a sense of safety. I could call on her for anything. Late night ride around, bonfires, and good times, we always had a good time. Life will never be the same without her, but I rest in the peace and hope of our Lord and Savior Jesus Christ.

Daryn added, “He is Healer, Miracle Worker, and Promise Keeper, and I’m holding onto the hope that one day we will be reunited in Him. I will forever miss you. Skyler and I are broken, what feels like right in two. We love you Kaslyn, I hope you know that. Friends and family, please keep the Switzer family and us in your prayers. This loss is catastrophic. May we meet again Kase!”

One person was taken to the hospital after a vehicle crashed into a building in Asheboro on Monday, according to the Asheboro Fire Department. Firefighters say that they arrived on scene to find someone trapped in a car that had driven on an embankment and partially into a Verizon building on Dixie Drive.

The front of the vehicle was around 8 feet off the ground. The person inside the car was in critical condition when they were extracted through the rear hatch.
