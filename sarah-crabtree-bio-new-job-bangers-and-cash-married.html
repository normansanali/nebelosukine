Meet Bangers And Cash's Sarah Crabtree! Bio, New Job, Is She Married?

July 2024 · 4 minute read

Meet Sarah Crabtree, a household name among Bangers and Cash fans.

You have seen the Yesterday show centered around Mathewson’s, a car-auction business. And other than the Mathewson’s, viewers were introduced to other cast members including Derek, Paul, Dave as well as Sarah. In 2022, though, viewers noticed that Sarah was nowhere to be seen.

So happened?

Find out in this ‘Sarah Crabtree Bio’.

Why Did Sarah Crabtree Leave Bangers And Cash?

Sarah Crabtree appeared as a cast member on docuseries Bangers and Cash from 2019 until 2021 according to her LinkedIn page. Given her passion for classic cars, it made sense why Sarah went on the show in the first place. But, why did she leave?

Sarah stopped becoming a part of Bangers and Cash as of May 2021. Around this time, she took to her social media to write “Series 4 of ‘Bangers’ has come to an end which means no more Crabbers on the telly box”.

As per her LinkedIn, she used to be an office manager for Mathewsons Classic Cars from November 2016 to October 2020. Be that as it may, Sarah also told Yesterday TV UK that she was at Mathewson’s since 1970. “Office Manager, a family friend and not to be messed with!”, the outlet introduced her to their audience. During her tenure, she aided the family auction business in Yorkshire in their charismatic and friendly family hunt for rare vehicles to sell. In the process, she also uncovered some remarkable and heart-warming stories behind the classic rides.

Sarah also left Bangers and Cash as she was instead working for the new Yesterday channel series called Secrets of the Transport Museum.

Sarah Crabtree New Job

As for her new job, Sarah Crabtree has been working as a sales director/brand ambassador of Evoke Classics, a motor vehicle company from 21 August 2021.

“She lives our core values; friendly, trustworthy, and approachable”, Sarah’s BIO on the Evoke website read. She has a team that works alongside her at the company, all of whom are proud of her. The company has also outlined its mission to make available a range of historically significant motor cars, all the best examples of the type that their clients will find for the given model and price.

As per LinkedIn, Sarah also formerly worked as a manager at The Chocolate Factory from September 2009 to November 2016.

As for her education, all we could say is, she graduated from Wolfreton School in 1986.

Is Sarah Crabtree Married?

Sarah Crabtree took to her Facebook to write she is ‘in a relationship since 6 July 2012’ and the status has not been updated for more than 5 years.

She has a seemingly-teenage daughter Lily Crabtree-Craft (on Instagram @lilycrabtree_) and a son named Solomon Red Craft.

Both of Sarah’s kids went to Lady Lumley’s School and her daughter alone studied at Leeds Arts University and worked as a waitress at The New Inn (at the time of this writing).

And because Sarah’s sister-in-law Carol Ann Stevens listed Sarah as sister-in-law and her daughter Lily as a niece, Sarah is still married. However, her husband was not known

Derek Mathewson is the founder and owner of Mathewson’s. So yes. for a long time, Sarah and Derek were related as costars on Bangers and Cash and as a boss and an employee when working at Mathewsons Classic Cars.
