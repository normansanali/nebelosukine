Robbie is the winner of Netflix’s cooking competition show Pressure Cooker. Along with 10 other participants, Robbie entered the tournament, which debuted on January 6th. He and his friends hosted a premiere party at North Wilmington of Bellefonte Brewing Co. to mark his appearance. In contrast to these shows, there is a “Survivor”-style element where the contestants evaluate one another. With a cool $100,000 up for grabs, winning requires skill and a cunning strategy that makes use of relationships.

Robbie was selected by the Delaware Business Times as one of the top 40 leaders under 40 and by the Delaware Restaurant Association as a Rising Star.
