Fallout 76 Grafton Monster Locations

Here, we will tell you where you can find the Grafton Monster in Fallout 76.

First Published March 10, 2023, 04:54

In Fallout 76, players are crafting items that will help them survive in Appalachia. Appalachia has become dangerous after the bombs were dropped in the area due for many reasons. One of the main reasons is that the nuclear fallout has mutated the animals in the area, and it has turned them into monsters.

One of the many monsters that players will likely run into is the Grafton Monster. The Grafton Monster is one of the many monsters players will be tasked to hunt for missions and even sometimes tasked to take a picture of for weekly challenges. Here, we are going to go over where players can find the Grafton Monster in Fallout 76.

Grafton Monster Location In Fallout 76

Finding the Grafton Monster in Fallout 76 is actually a lot easier than you might think it is. There are a couple of locations in Appalachia where the Grafton Monster has a 100% spawn rate. Here are the guaranteed locations for finding the Grafton Monster:

  • Charleston Trainyard
  • Charleston Capitol Building

Here are some locations where the Grafton Monster could spawn:

  • Hemlock Holes
  • Toxic Dried Lakebed

Just remember, for any of these locations, the Grafton Monster could have been already killed by other players. If you are having trouble finding the Grafton Monster, you should try server hopping to hopefully find the Grafton Monster.

Is It Worth Hunting Down The Grafton Monster?

There are a couple of reasons why players might want to find and hunt down the Grafton Monster. The first is to complete weekly events. Finding the Grafton Monster could help with weekly or daily tasks, which will give players more SCORE points which will give them even more season rewards.

Another reason is to get crafting components. The Grafton Monster will drop both oil and acid, two important components for crafting. Acid is used to break down materials like steel so they can be crafted into weapons and armor, two very important items in Fallout 76. Oil is used to craft mechanical devices. As you can see, there are plenty of reasons why players might want to go find the Grafton Monster.
