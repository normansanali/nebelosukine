A Look Inside Wanda Sykes' Personal Life And Net Worth

Wanda Sykes is a stand-up comedian, actress and writer. Sykes was first recognized for her work as a writer on The Chris Rock Show. She won a Primetime Emmy for her work in 1999. As an actress you may know her from The New Adventures of Old Christine, Curb Your Enthusiasm, Black-ish, Evan Almighty, License To Wed and IceAge: Collision Course, among others.

Although fans know her as one of the funniest people in America, they might not know much about her personal life. Celebrities tend to keep work and life separate. Although she does share some stuff with her fans, mostly through stories in her stand-up, Sykes does tend to keep her personal life private. She was just announced as one of the host for 2022's Oscars. Let's find out a bit more about her before she takes on hosting duties. Here is a look inside Wanda Sykes' personal life and current net worth.

8 Wanda Sykes' Early Family Life

Wanda Sykes was born on March 7, 1964, in Portsmouth, Virginia. When she was in third grade, her family moved to Maryland. Sykes' mother, Marion Louise, worked as a banker, and her father, Harry, was a U.S. Army Colonel. She had an older brother named Harry. Sykes attended high school in Maryland and then went on to attend Hampton University, where she earned a degree in marketing.

7 Wanda Sykes' First Marriage

Sykes married record producer Dave Hall in 1991. Hall is known for working with artists like Madonna and Mary J. Blige. Sykes and Hall remained married until 1998. The two have never really spoken about why the marriage ended. She did, however, poke fun at her marriage during her stand-up routines, which maybe weren't all in good fun. Years later, Sykes told Oprah Winfrey that she had "felt little connection with her husband" during their marriage.

6 Wanda Sykes' Coming Out Story

One major reason she might have ended her marriage to Hall is because Sykes is a lesbian. In 2008, at the age of 44, Sykes publicly came out. Sykes attended an equality rally in Las Vegas during that year and let the world know how she really identified. She came out to her parents when she was 40, who were both very conservative. They refused to attend her to wedding to her wife and had a falling out with their daughter because of it, but they have all since reconciled.

5 Her Second Marriage

Wanda Sykes met Alex Niedbalski in 2006. She first spotted Niedbalski on a ferry ride to Fire Island in New York. A friend formerly introduced them and the rest was history. Niedbalski, a French woman, sold countertops at the time. Just about one month before Sykes attended the same-sex rally in 2008, she and Alex were married without anyone knowing. They are set to celebrate their 14th anniversary this year.

4 Wanda Sykes And Alex Niedbalski's Children

Neither Wanda nor Alex have shared pictures of their children on social media, but according to PEOPLE, the couple welcomed fraternal twins in 2009. Neidbalski carried the twins, and they were born on April 27 of that year. They had a son, Lucas Claude, and a daughter, Olivia Lou. They will be 13 years old this April.

3 Wanda Sykes' Life-Changing Diagnosis

In September 2011, Wanda Sykes appeared on The Ellen DeGeneres Show and announced she had been diagnosed with ductal carcinoma in situ (DCIS) in her left breast, which is a zero-stage breast cancer. However, to make sure she didn't get breast cancer in the future, Sykes underwent a double mastectomy. It began when she went in to get a routine breast reduction surgery, she explained.

2 Where She Spends Most Of Her Time

The Sykes' split their time between Los Angeles, California, and Media, Pennsylvania. She likes the hustle and bustle of busy city life and also to get away sometimes to a small, quiet town, according to an interview with The Philadelphia Inquirer. Sykes and her wife show support to the sports teams in both states. Besides liking the quietness and simplicity of it, it is unknown why Sykes picked Media to settle in, since she was born in Virginia and lived most of her life in Maryland.

1 Wanda Sykes' Current Net Worth

Between acting, writing, and performing stand-up comedy, Wanda Sykes has made a great career for herself. According to Celebrity Net Worth,her current net worth is $10 million. She has more than 70 acting credits to her name, including TV, film and voice acting. Another form of income for Sykes was money from her 2004 book, "Yeah, I Said It."
