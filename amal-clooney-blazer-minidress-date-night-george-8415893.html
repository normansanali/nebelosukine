Amal Clooney Is Our Date-Night Style Muse in a Blazer and Minidress

July 2024 · 2 minute read

It doesn’t matter what the occasion is, Amal Clooney’s wardrobe always impresses — be it a glitzy disco number, a polished pinstripe jumpsuit, or a slinky slip dress — after all, she is the mastermind behind her wardrobe. And on Wednesday night, the fashion icon proved her style has range, swapping her evening gowns for the ultimate date-night outfit.

After starting the week off on a sartorial high note in a lemon-colored Versace gown, Amal continued her parade of very good looks while heading to the Polo Bar in New York City, arm-in-arm with her husband, George Clooney. Giving everyone the one outfit they need for any and every date night, the human rights lawyer slipped into a blush pink feathered minidress, which she layered underneath a black satin blazer. Amal accessorized with a pair of metallic pointed-toe pumps, a black clutch, and diamond drop earrings.

When it came time for glam, Amal opted for her signature glowing complexion with feathered lashes, shimmery eyelids, rosy cheeks, and glossy lips while she styled her long brunette hair sleek and straight with curled ends and a side part.

For George’s part, he changed speeds and traded his trousers for a pair of jeans, which he teamed with a black button-up and a gray moto jacket.

Just a few days ago, it was revealed that Amal is the sole creator behind all of her stellar outfits. In a new interview, George outed his wife for not having a stylist while speaking with Access Hollywood. “I’ll put on an outfit, and she’ll come downstairs, and I’m embarrassed,” he jokingly shared, adding: “You know, my wife doesn’t have a stylist. She does it all on her own.”
