Dorthea Lauren Allegra Lapkus is an American actress, comedian, voice-over artist, and an impressionist. She is best known for playing the role of ‘Susan Fischer’ in the Netflix series “Orange Is the New Black” and ‘Jess’ in the HBO series “Crashing.”

Wiki/Biography

Lauren Lapkus was born on Friday, September 6, 1985 (age 34 years; as in 2019) in Evanston, Illinois, U.S. Her zodiac sign is Virgo.

Lauren Lapkus in childhood

She did her schooling from Illinois‘ Evanston Township High School. Lauren grew up watching many television sitcoms and shows like “Saturday Night Live” and “In Living Color.” She started filming sketches with her elder brother at an early age. While in school, Lauren participated in the annual student revenue and variety show, YAMO.

Lauren Lapkus in teenage

Later, she took improv classes at the iO Theatre in Chicago. Lauren graduated in English from the DePaul University. After her graduation, Lauren moved to New York City  where she joined the Upright Citizens Brigade Theatre. After around an year, she relocated to Los Angeles.

Husband & Relationships

Lauren Lapkus got married to the actor and improviser, Chris Alvarado, on May 3, 2014. The couple announced their separation on February 14, 2016.

After her divorce, Lauren started dating the American actor, Mike Castle. The couple tied the knot on October 5, 2018.

Career

Lauren Lapkus started her career in 2011 as a model by featuring in a number of TV commercials like “Snickers” and “Jack In The Box.” She made her acting debut in 2005 with the film, “Movie Boy” in which she played the role of ‘Pamela.’ Subsequently, she featured in the short films, “Dog DNA,” “Secrets, Secrets,” “Plastic Heart,” and “Manhattan Mixup.” Thereafter, she went on to work in films like ” Bread and Butter,” “Jurassic World,” “Girlfriend’s Day,” “The Unicorn,” and “Holmes & Watson.”

Lauren Lapkus in The Unicorn

She made her TV debut in 2007 with the television film, “Carpeted Afterhours.” Next, she appeared in TV shows like “Jimmy Kimmel Live!,” “The Back Room,” “Are You There, Chelsea?,” “The Earliest Show,” and “House of Lies.”

Lauren Lapkus in The Earliest Show

She gained immense popularity after playing the role of ‘Susan Fischer’ in the TV series, “Orange Is the New Black.”

Lauren Lapkus in Orange Is the New Black

Besides films and TV serials, she has also appeared on many podcasts, including “Womp It Up!,” “If I Were You,” “Thrilling Adventure Hour,” “Gilmore Guys,” and “Comedy Bang! Bang!.” She also has her own podcast, “With Special Guest Lauren Lapkus.”
