In January 2020, it was discovered that Carlsen had been dating University of Oslo alumna Elisabet Lorentzen Djnne for a year. On New Year’s Day of that year, the chess champion uploaded a series of images to Instagram, one of which included Djnne.

“It’s been a terrific year on and off the board, on to the next!” he captioned the photo. According to Chess Base, Carlsen’s partner Djnne is from Hardanger in Western Norway. In terms of age, she is five years younger than Carlsen.

However, they have not been seen together in public for a considerable amount of time. The couple announced their separation at the end of March 2021.

His Chess Profession and Rise to the Top

Carlsen has constantly demonstrated that he is the best chess player by winning over 300 rated tournament games and multiple blitz competitions. In 2004, he made headlines when he won Group C at the Corus Chess Tournament in Wijk aan Zee. In March of the same year, he defeated former world champion Anatoly Karpov in a blitz chess event.

When he won the ‘International Chess Festival Biel Grandmaster Tournament’ in August 2007, he became the youngest player ever to win a category 18 tournament. FIDE named Carlsen the greatest chess player in the world in 2010.
