What is the Storyline of WWE 2K24?

August 2024 Â· 3 minute read

2024 will witness the launch of WWE 2K24, the latest episode of the enduring series of professional wrestling video games. Developed by 2K games, the game is the largest in the franchise to date, promising a more comprehensive slate of current WWE and Legends wrestlers and a wide range of match modes.

Let's discuss how the expected storylines and actions may unfold differently in the game.

WWE 2K24 boasts the most comprehensive roster of over 200 WWE Superstars and Legends to date. Cody Rhodes, Bianca Belair, and Rhea Ripley are among the returning favorites as the official cover Superstars for WWE 2K24. Another similar game mode includes the tribute to WrestleMania in 2K Showcase with the stars of the Immortals, alongside My RISE, My FACTION, My GM, and new match forms such as the Guest Referee and Gauntlet.

The assorted roster professionals promise to add a new dimension to the game's tale, inviting designers to communicate with familiar faces and retailers.

2K Showcase: WrestleMania Tribute

Retelling the greatest moments in WrestleMania history within its 2K Showcase mode, WWE does not find any struggle too hard to narrate over. With many legendary matches and rivalries from this storied event, players can experience nostalgia for sports entertainment. This mode adds layers to the game's narrative, giving players a mechanism by which they can play as their favorite WWE Superstars and do battle in some of pro wrestling's greatest moments.

My FACTION Mode & Collectible Cards

WWE 2K24 will feature the all-new My FACTION mode, which allows players to collect cards and assemble a faction featuring WWE Superstars and legends throughout any era. Before going into apocalyptic territory control, players can call their shots and even create games' narratives on a scale of large rivalry or small-scale storyline. 

Post-Launch DLC Packs

Post-Launch DLC Packs includes more than 20 additional content spread out over five different post-launch DLC. Including these DLC packs bolsters the game's worldbuilding and introduces players to new characters and stories that have been written beyond its initial release. The game's narrative is set to continue after launch, and this post-release content makes the story interesting, providing, additional chances for players to enjoy new plot points and rivalries.

Featuring a diverse and regularly updated roster of Superstars, multiple match types, variants like Broadway-style Iron Man contests, and the ability to create your wrestlers, coupled with the ability to launch DLC drops, might promise some thick narrative meat for WWE Universe storytelling.

With the Wrestle Mania Tribute in 2K Showcase of the Immortals, My FACTION strategy mode, and returning legendary Superstars, WWE 2K24 offers plenty of storytelling possibilities to explore. Throughout the ongoing game, players must do significant plot and character arc stuff while taking in more of that good professional wrestling.
