Is Simon Le Bon Still Married to Yasmin?: Simon Le Bon, also known as Simon John Charles Le Bon is an English singer and songwriter.

He developed a passion for singing at a younger age, has been consistent throughout his career, and has become one of the most sought-after English singers.

Simon Le Bon is best known as the lead vocalist and lyricist of the new wave band; Duran Duran and its offshoot, Arcadia

He’s a recipient of several awards, including three Ivor Novello Awards from the British Academy of Songwriters, Composers, and Authors, with one recognizing his Outstanding Contribution to British Music.

Is Simon Le Bon Still Married to Yasmin?

Yes, Simon Le Bon is still happily married to Yasmin Le Bon. They tied the knot on Friday, 27 December 1985, and their enduring marriage has spanned nearly 35 years.
