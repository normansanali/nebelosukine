Billy Porter's Best Red Carpet Fashion, Beauty Moments

Credit: David Fisher/Shutterstock

Wings! Feathers! Fringe! Look Back at Billy Porter’s Most Show-Stopping Red Carpet Moments

Strike a pose! When it comes to the red carpet, Billy Porter never disappoints — ever. Because whether he’s rolling up to a big awards show like the Oscars or a more intimate affair like the GLAAD Media Awards, the actor knows exactly how to make headlines.

[ami-related id="1320983" url="https://www.usmagazine.com/stylish/pictures/beyonces-red-carpet-style-evolution-since-destinys-child-pics/" title="Look Back at Beyonce’s Most Iconic Fashion Moments" target="_blank" thumb="false" imgsrc="https://www.usmagazine.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/08/Beyonces-Latest-Versace-Look-Serves-Major-Barbie-Doll-Vibes.jpg?quality=86&strip=all" imgid="2090315" format="gallery" flag="" channel="33"]

Because regardless of if he’s rocking insanely tall platform shoes, an absurdly amazing beauty look or blinged out to the max, the Pose actor certainly knows how to make a splash with his style. 

Rewind the clock to the 2021 Emmys, and Porter shut down the red carpet from the second he stepped on the scene. Wearing a custom ensemble from Ashi Studio, complete with wings no less, glitzy ear cuffs and a statement necklace from Lorraine Schwartz and heels courtesy of Harry Halim, Porter served up a whole freakin’ look. 

The actor doesn’t always stick to a pair pants though. He can rock a dress like no other. Take his outfit at the 2020 Oscars for example. He showed up in a printed Giles Deacon dress worthy of an award. 

[ami-related id="6652" url="https://www.usmagazine.com/stylish/pictures/see-natalie-portmans-best-red-carpet-transformations-w468068/" title="Look Back at Natalie Portman’s Beauty Evolution" target="_blank" thumb="false" imgsrc="https://www.usmagazine.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/06/Natalie-Portman-Turns-40-Look-Back-At-Her-Beauty-Evolution-Slide-2.jpg?quality=86&strip=all" imgid="2042395" format="gallery" flag="" channel="33"]

Perhaps one his most memorable fashion moment however came to pass at the 2019 Academy Awards, when he shut down the red carpet custom tuxedo ball gown created by Christian Siriano. The velvet piece was voluminous, breathtaking and all-around amazing.

[sendtonews type="float" key="frrO9lGp38-2839882-14453"]

Earlier that year, he proved that he had a penchant for color, rocking a pink and red number  to the 2019 Tony Awards. The satin and tulle outfit featured 30,000 Swarovski crystals as well as recycled fabric from a curtain backdrop from Broadway's Kinky Boots, for which he won a Best Actor Tony in 2013. That detail made the ensemble not just gorgeous, but nostalgic and sustainable, to boot!

In addition to his daring fashion choices, Porter also turns well-coiffed heads with his bold beauty moves — and we want more! 

[ami-related id="894653" url="https://www.usmagazine.com/stylish/pictures/selena-gomezs-best-red-carpet-moments-and-style-evolution-w507167/" title="Selena Gomez’s All-Time Best Style Moments: See Her Fashion Evolution" target="_blank" thumb="false" imgsrc="https://www.usmagazine.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/04/Selena-Gomez-Shares-More-Details-On-Rare-Beauty-Promo.jpg?quality=86&strip=all" imgid="1769921" format="gallery" flag="" channel="33"]

At the 25th annual Critics’ Choice Awards for example, he rocked a lime green mani, which was created by Vanessa Sanchez McCullough, with a broken mirror effect that popped perfectly against his silver statement rings. That’s not all though. His arms and chest were also covered in beautiful butterfly tattoos! 

To see his best beauty and fashion moments of all time, keep scrolling. Us Weekly’s Stylish is rounding up Porter's best red carpet moments, from blinged-out fingers to drop-dead gorgeous feathered trains.
